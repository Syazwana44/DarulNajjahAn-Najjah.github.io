AN-NAJJAH AIMS TO:

1. Educate and develop boys to become a family leader who can lead the family and then can become a National leader in the future.

2. Providing a platform that gives the community an opportunity to do charity by offering donations/help and support so that this Care Center will continue to be administered perfectly.

3. Helping the less fortunate from among Single Mothers, the Poor and the Disabled by organizing awareness and leadership programs in collaboration with government agencies. Provide livelihood assistance to this target group (this activity has started back in 2001).

4. Efforts are being made to raise funds to establish a Dyslexia Center and a Community Center to be used by the target groups in the AN-NAJJAH assistance program.

AN-NAJJAH VOLUNTEERS

The AN-NAJJAH Management welcomes individuals who are interested in realizing the goals of AN-NAJJAH by contributing energy, skills and financial support to this target group.
