The best gift of Allah s.w.t. that's when we can share with those who are in dire need. For those blessed by Allah s.w.t. with knowledge and utilize that knowledge by spreading it. Those who have physical strength, use that strength by helping those who are weak. While those who have abundant property use it by giving alms, donating, and zakat. Remember, there will be no shortage of wealth that you spend except for Allah s.w.t. will double it to a number we cannot count. Brothers and sisters, If you want goodness in this world and the hereafter, we invite you to sponsor the children under our supervision to become memorizers of the Al-Quran. We at DARUL NAJJAH are very welcome to your donations. Hopefully, the donations you give can further increase the number of huffaz born among orphans. For your information, we are here to provide free services to orphans and poor children who come from all over the country. We are very pleased to receive donations in any form so that they can be used for the children here. Perhaps one day we will be able to build our own building and accept more children to become memorizers of the Al-Quran and be able to develop knowledge - their knowledge throughout the country.

An-Najjah was established on the 28th of June 2006. The main activity of AN-NAJJAH is to manage an AN-NAJJAH Orphan / New Brother Care Center which is under its auspices based at No. 3, Jalan Kent 2, Off Jalan Maktab, 54000 Kuala Lumpur.
